02 · How It Works
The Intelligence Pipeline

From global noise to tactical clarity — in five stages.

Every stage feeds the next. Billions of daily signals are collected, deduplicated, scored and distilled into the specific insight and ready-to-send response your team needs.

01

Collect

RSS, news APIs, social listeners, search engines and polite web crawlers ingest continuously into one normalized schema.

02

Analyze

Deduplication, language detection, sentiment and entity extraction clean and score the firehose in real time.

03

Detect

Trend spikes, crisis severity, journalist influence and newsjacking windows surface automatically.

04

Alert

Severity-graded alerts via email, Slack, Teams, Telegram and webhooks — before the story breaks.

05

Act

AI drafts talking points, holding statements and counter-narratives in your organization's voice.

Face-Scan Check-In

Org-Scoped Security

Native Companion
